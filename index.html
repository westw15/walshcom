Walsh Commercial Real Estate

Walsh Commercial Real Estate is an independent, professional real estate company offering specialized services in the sale and leasing of office, health care, medical-specialty offices, and retail real estate, including the brokerage of land for development, quality investment property, and client-specific advisory services. Walsh Commercial Real Estate’s primary geographic area of operation is the Philadelphia Metropolitan Area and the neighboring communities located throughout southeast Pennsylvania. Our main office is strategically located in Horsham, Pennsylvania. Core Professional Real Estate Services include:

  • Facility Sales and Leasing
  • Buyer/Tenant Representation
  • Investment Sales and Acquisitions
  • Advisory Services

Walsh Commercial Real Estate takes great pride in providing professional real estate services personalized to the needs and objectives of each client. We look forward to the opportunity of working with you and satisfying your unique real estate requirements.

Sales and Leasing

Walsh Commercial Real Estate effectively markets office, health care, medical-specialty, retail, land for development, and multi-family investment properties for sale and commercial lease using aggressive and targeted campaigns designed to maximize value for our clients. Whether buying, selling, or leasing, a single transaction or a diverse property portfolio, each Walsh Commercial Action Plan is customized to address the specific and unique needs of the client and implemented to ensure that the real estate goals support the business objectives. Our business is maximizing value for our clients. We take great pride in building long lasting, mutually beneficial, and rewarding relationships based on our real estate expertise, honest straight forward communication, and commitment to exceptional customer service.

Representation

Walsh Commercial Real Estate provides customized real estate representation services to purchasers and users of office, health care, medical-specialty offices, retail, land for development, and multi-family properties. Every Buyer or Commercial Tenant Representation Action Plan is designed to ensure that the client’s needs and objectives are satisfied to their highest expectations. Walsh Commercial employs a client-centered, multi-step Action Plan to ensure that our client’s business objectives are aligned with their real estate goals. Given the complexities of today’s business requirements and the growing impact that real estate has on the bottom line, we aggressively evaluate all alternatives, so the final lease, purchase, sale, or re-capitalization decisions result in reduced risk, reduced costs, maximized profitability, and high client satisfaction. Additional customized Buyer/Tenant Representation services include Strategic Planning, Transaction Management, and Real Estate Administration Services.

Strategic Planning:

Assignment Analysis; Target Market(s) and Vocational Studies; Site Specific Demographic Analysis; Traffic Studies, Space Planning with Facility Consultation, and Strategic Real Estate Plan Development and Implementation

Transaction Management Services:

Site Selection, Real Estate Acquisitions, Real Estate Dispositions, Sale/Lease-back Packaging, and a variety of Financial Structuring, including 1031 Exchange and Reverse Exchange Transactions.

Real Estate Administration Services:

Lease Administration/Abstracts, Project Management, Property Management, and Due Diligence Investigations

Investments
